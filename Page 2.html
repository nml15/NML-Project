Chemistry in Daily Life

Chemistry impacts our lives in countless ways, from cleaning products to the foods we eat.

Chemistry in Food

  • Cooking: Heat causes chemical reactions like caramelization.
  • Preservation: Salt and vinegar prevent food spoilage.
  • Nutrition: Proteins, vitamins, and minerals are chemical compounds.

Chemistry in Cleaning

  • Soaps: Contain surfactants that remove grease and dirt.
  • Bleach: Breaks down stains and kills bacteria.
  • Vinegar: Acetic acid acts as a natural cleaning agent.

Chemistry in Medicine

  • Drugs: Painkillers, antibiotics, and other medicines are chemical formulations.
  • Vaccines: Use chemical compounds to boost immunity.
  • Sanitizers: Kill germs using alcohol and antiseptics.

Chemistry in Personal Care

  • Shampoo: Contains surfactants to clean hair.
  • Toothpaste: Fluoride helps strengthen teeth.
  • Makeup: A blend of chemical compounds for beauty products.

Chemistry in Environment

  • Air: Oxygen, nitrogen, and carbon dioxide are vital gases.
  • Water Treatment: Chlorine purifies water for safe consumption.
  • Fertilizers: Provide essential nutrients for plants.
